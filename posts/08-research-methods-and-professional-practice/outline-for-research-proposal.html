Outline for Research Proposal

Presentation outline proposal - Unit 8

Tuesday, 18 March 2025, 14:30 PM

The word count for a minimum 15-minute presentation will be 1,500 words and a maximum of 2,250 words.

Project title:
Cyberbullying, how serious is it, and what impact does it have in the UK?

Presentation Structure:
The presentation will be created using Microsoft PowerPoint. An MP3 video will be provided along with a YouTube link, and the submission will also include an audio script. The presentation will aim for a maximum of 20 to 30 slides, with a minimum of 10; it should be visually engaging and content-rich while avoiding excessive text. I will handle the narration, but my picture will not appear on the slide.

Introduction: (300 words)
This section will first introduce what cyberbullying is and how it has evolved from traditional school bullying into the online realm. It will provide an overview of the context to be discussed alongside the research question. The introduction will also clarify that it will not investigate specific areas related to cyberbullying, such as child grooming, racial hatred, and issues concerning religious beliefs or sexual orientation, as the aim is to provide a general overview of the content surrounding cyberbullying.

Research question: (1200 words)
The research will investigate the current data on the prevalence of cyberbullying, primarily occurring on social media platforms. It will assess the size of these platforms in relation to the global population and explore the rising trend of cyberbullying worldwide. Given that social media operates globally, it is crucial to consider the implications of its international reach, as cyberbullying can emerge from various jurisdictions.

Furthermore, the research will analyse UK legislation and laws addressing cyberbullying, as well as review several case studies of instances where cyberbullying led to individuals taking their own lives due to online harassment.

The research will also be required to analyse the data and critically assess UK legislation.

Conclusion: (500 words)
Depending on the research, it may indicate that the UK government is taking the necessary measures to tackle cyberbullying and that social media platforms are implementing methods to combat it, or the research may state that not enough is being done and that the UK government should introduce new methods to reduce cyberbullying.
