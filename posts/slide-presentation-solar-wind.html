The Solar Winds Breach Case Study

Unit 2 Seminar - Network Security

Friday, 17 November 2023, 12:00 PM

During Dr. Khan's seminar, the Solar Winds Breach presentation slides were shared with the students. To participate in the discussion of security issues in computer networks, the students were required to read two articles: "A 'Worst Nightmare' Cyberattack: The Untold Story Of The SolarWinds Hack" and "Intelligence-Driven Computer Network Defense Informed by Analysis of Adversary Campaigns and Intrusion Kill Chains." In addition, students were asked to cover legal, social, ethical, and professional concerns using appropriate tools and techniques. The language used in these discussions should be simple and direct, and prioritise important information using active voice.
