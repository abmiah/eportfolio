It is of utmost importance that Pampered Pets comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) guidelines, particularly in their transition to the digital sphere. Any transactions carried out within the European Union (EU), including the United Kingdom, must strictly abide by these regulations.

The handling of personal information.
This is any information that identifies an individual.

Data protection principles.
The way data is stored and the type of information stored must be transparent to users and secure. Organisations are instructed to keep only necessary information.

Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIAs).
Organisations should analyse data to identify any vulnerabilities, the risk of data breach, and the impact that may have on individuals.

Data Sharing.
Data sharing should only be actioned if required; for this case of Pampered Pets, no data sharing should be implemented.

It is noteworthy that the fulfilment and sustenance of ISO 27001 certification requisites can effectively cater to the GDPR data processing security prerequisites. This implies that a thorough ISO certification, encompassing stress testing and staff training, can ensure complete and comprehensive data privacy and security for your organisation.
