Developing Your Proposal – Outline

Activity - Unit 1

Monday, 5 May 2025, 21:48 PM

Research area and working title:
The Ethics of Data Science Companies Influencing or Manipulating an Audience in a Political Election.

Proposed Research Problem and Research Question:
This will examine the ethical aspects of data companies harbouring vast quantities of user data to influence and sway them toward a particular political agenda. This will investigate the case of Cambridge Analytica and data abuse, aligning with the CyBOK framework regarding human, organisational, and regulatory aspects.

Proposed Aims and Objectives:
The objective is to explore how specific agendas can readily sway individuals through the collection or dependence on user data and how social media platforms have become instruments for promoting political beliefs. Do social media companies hold any responsibility for influencing users towards a political agenda, and do they consider the security of users' data when third-party applications access it?

Proposed Research Design:
This will examine the infamous Cambridge Analytica and how it used data to influence its alleged clients' political views. It will explore the platforms utilised and various data points collected to gather users’ information to advance the client's agenda.

Artefact(s) that can be created:
Various data tables showcase the types of data gathered and whether they target particular groups, indicating if those groups are more likely to be influenced by the campaign. This will also display technical code for mitigation while verifying if these campaigns are legitimate by searching through credible sources.
